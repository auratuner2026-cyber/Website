Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 24, 2026

The short version

Aura Tuner collects no data. Zero. Nothing leaves your device.

Data We Collect

Aura Tuner does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app operates entirely on your device with no network connectivity required or used for its core functionality.

  • ✓No account required
  • ✓No analytics or crash reporting
  • ✓No advertising identifiers
  • ✓No third-party SDKs that collect data

Microphone Access

Aura Tuner requests microphone access solely to detect pitch and analyze audio in real time. Audio is processed locally on your device using Apple's Core Audio framework and is never recorded, stored, or transmitted anywhere.

System Audio (macOS)

On macOS, Aura Tuner may optionally capture system audio using Apple's ScreenCaptureKit framework. This audio is processed exclusively in memory for real-time analysis and is never saved or transmitted.

Children's Privacy

Aura Tuner does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The app fully complies with COPPA.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this privacy policy, we will post the new version on this page and update the "last updated" date above.
