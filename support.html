Frequently Asked Questions

Why does the app need microphone access?

Aura Tuner needs microphone access to detect the pitch of your instrument in real time. Audio is processed entirely on your device ‚Äî it's never recorded or sent anywhere.

Does Aura Tuner work without internet?

Yes ‚Äî 100%. Aura Tuner is fully offline. No internet connection is required or used at any point.

How do I capture system audio on Mac?

Tap the input selector and choose "System Audio". You may be prompted to grant Screen Recording permission ‚Äî this is required by macOS for system audio capture and is used only to analyze audio in real time.

How do I change the reference pitch?

Open Settings (the gear icon), then drag the Reference Pitch slider. You can set A4 anywhere from 432 Hz to 446 Hz. The default is 440 Hz.

Are there any in-app purchases or subscriptions?

No. Aura Tuner is a one-time download with no in-app purchases, no subscriptions, and no ads. Everything is included from day one.

The tuner isn't detecting my instrument. What can I do?

Make sure microphone access is granted in Settings ‚Üí Privacy ‚Üí Microphone. Play one clear note at a time close to the mic, and check that the correct preset is selected for your instrument.
